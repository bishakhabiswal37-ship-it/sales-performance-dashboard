# Retail Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive sales analytics dashboard built to track revenue trends, regional performance, and top-selling products across a full fiscal year.

## Overview

This dashboard analyzes 5,400+ retail orders (Jan–Dec 2025) to answer common business questions:
- How is revenue trending month over month?
- Which regions and product categories drive the most sales?
- What are the top-performing products?
- How does discounting affect order volume and revenue?

## Features

- **KPI summary** — total revenue, order count, average order value, units sold
- **Monthly revenue trend** — line chart showing seasonal patterns (e.g. festive-season spike in Q4)
- **Revenue by region & category** — comparative bar charts
- **Region × Category heatmap** — spot where specific product categories perform best geographically
- **Top 10 products table** — ranked by revenue with unit counts
- **Interactive filters** — drill down by region and category

## Tools & Approach

- **Data modeling:** SQL-style aggregation (grouping, summing, filtering) to turn raw transaction-level data into dashboard-ready summaries
- **Visualization:** Custom-built interactive charts (SVG-based) styled for clarity and fast scanning — mirrors the kind of BI dashboard I'd build in Power BI for a client
- **Data:** Synthetic dataset generated to reflect realistic retail patterns (seasonality, regional demand differences, category mix)
